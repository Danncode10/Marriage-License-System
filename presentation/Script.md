### **Slide 3: Introduction**
**(Read the slide)**
*   **Problem:** Manual processing is slow, prone to errors, and lacks real-time tracking.
*   **Solution:** A modern, centralized, role-based system.

**Speech:**
"Let’s be honest: manual encoding is a headache. It’s slow, it’s hard to find old records, and applicants keep calling to ask, 'Sir/Ma'am, ano na pong status ng application ko?' Our system solves this by centralizing everything. No more lost papers, no more manual errors, and most importantly, both the staff and the applicants can see exactly what is happening in one click."

---

### **Slide 4: Available In**
**(Read the slide)**
*   Computer Version: Side-by-Side workflow and precision.
*   Mobile Version: Portability and convenience.

**Speech:**
"One of our biggest advantages is accessibility. The staff at MCRO can use the Computer Version for faster typing and side-by-side verification of documents. But we also have a Mobile Version. This is perfect for applicants who want to check their status while they are at work or on the go. Our system is ready whenever and wherever you need it."

---

### **Slide 5: UI and UX**
**(Read the slide)**
*   Clean and modern design, easy to use, simple dropdowns for a smooth experience.

**Speech:**
"We made sure the system doesn't look 'complicated.' It features a clean, modern design—just like the apps we use daily like Facebook or Shopee. We used simple dropdown menus and clear buttons so that even those who aren't tech-savvy can learn how to use it in just a few minutes."

---

### **Slide 6: Roles and Access**
**(Read the slide)**
*   Applicant: Own submission only.
*   Employee: Processing and verification.
*   Admin: System oversight and staff management.

**Speech:**
"Control is important. We have three levels of access. Applicants can only see their own application. Employees can process and verify documents but cannot delete system data. And the Admin has full oversight, including the ability to manage the staff and download global reports."

---

### **Slide 7: Applicant/User**
**(Read the slide)**
*   Fill up online before going to MCRO.
*   See requirements list.
*   Track application status online.

**Speech:**
"Here is the game-changer for the citizens. Instead of the applicant spending hours at the MCRO office just to fill up a form, they can do it AT HOME on their phone. They can see the list of requirements immediately, so when they finally visit the LGU, they are 100% prepared. It makes the visit faster, with less 'hustle' for everyone."

---

### **Slide 8: Employee**
**(Read the slide)**
*   Create walk-in applications.
*   View, edit, download, and set status.
*   Take photos and give registry numbers.

**Speech:**
"Now, for our hard-working employees. We know that not everyone has an email or wants to apply online. That’s okay! Our system allows employees to create 'Walk-in' accounts for people even without an email address. 

And here is a very special feature: We noticed that before, employees would take a photo of a document using their phone, send it to Messenger, download it to a PC, and then put it in Excel. That is too many steps! In our system, you just open the camera on your phone within the app, take a 'snap,' and it is automatically saved to the record. One snap, and it’s done. No more Messenger, no more manual Excel editing."

---

### **Slide 9: What can Admin Do?**
**(Read the slide)**
*   Full control, staff management, and download reports.
*   Analytics: Address distribution, religion, age, and FIFO/FILO sorting.

**Speech:**
"For the Admin, we provide 'One-Click Decision Making.' Need a report for the Mayor? Just click a button. You can see analytics—like which barangay has the most applicants, the age distribution of couples, or their religious affiliations. You can sort them by 'First In, First Out' to make sure the oldest applications are handled first. It’s not just a database; it’s a tool for better governance."

---

### **Slide 10: Data Privacy & Compliance**
**(Read the slide)**
*   Republic Act 10173 compliance.
*   Data Privacy Notice popup for applicants.
*   Secure storage and Role-Based Access.

**Speech:**
"We take safety seriously. Even before an applicant starts typing on their phone, a Data Privacy Notice pops up. This ensures we are following RA 10173 or the Data Privacy Act of 2012. We make sure the citizens know their data is safe, and we make sure the LGU is legally protected."

---

### **Slide 11: Backend & Database**
**(Read the slide)**
*   Backend as a Service (Supabase) + PostgreSQL.
*   Email confirmations and real-time syncing.
*   Cloud hosting via Vercel.

**Speech:**
"Under the hood, we use industry-standard tech. We use PostgreSQL—the most reliable database system. We use Supabase to handle the backend and emailing. This means when an account is created, the system can automatically send a confirmation email. Everything is synced in real-time. If an employee updates a status on their computer, the applicant sees it on their phone INSTANTLY."

---

### **Slide 12: Security Measures**
**(Read the slide)**
*   Supabase Authentication (Secure login and email verification).
*   Row Level Security (RLS) to prevent hacking.
*   Input validation (No SQL injection).

**Speech:**
"To the IT department, rest assured the system is secure. We use Row Level Security—this means even if someone tries to 'hack' the link, the database will block them unless they have the right permission. We also have strict input validation to prevent malicious code from entering our records."
